Evening Examen

St. Ignatius of Loyola · Five quiet minutes

1 of 5 · Gratitude
Where did I feel God's presence today?
Rest in gratitude. Notice the gifts — large and small — of this day.
2 of 5 · Review
Walk through your day with God.
Where did you feel consolation? Where desolation? Where were you most alive?
3 of 5 · Honesty
Where did you fall short?
Not to condemn — to see clearly. Where were you less than your best self?
4 of 5 · Forgiveness
Make an act of contrition.
Ask God for forgiveness and the grace to do better. Receive His mercy.
5 of 5 · Tomorrow
Look to tomorrow with hope.
One grace to ask for. One thing to offer. Go in peace.
