Virtue of the Month

Why virtue?

Neuroscience calls it self-directed neuroplasticity; the Church calls it habitus. A virtue is a stable disposition carved by repeated free acts — every deliberate act of patience strengthens the prefrontal pathways that make the next act easier. Grace builds on nature.
