                                    How It Works

                                    🏠 Home
                                    Daily greetingChanges morning / afternoon / evening automatically.
                                    Today's ScheduleAuto-builds your day from your events as anchors, then slots your tasks into the gaps by duration. Faith rhythm tasks appear here too.
                                    Progress ringShows how many of today's tasks are done.
                                    Overdue alertRed banner appears if you have a one-time task past its due date.
                                    CountdownsEdit any date by clicking it. Days and months calculate automatically.
                                    This Week's FocusFree-form priority list. Add anything, check off, delete with ✕.
                                    Switch personTop right avatars — switches whose tasks and schedule you see. Rename yourselves in Settings.
                                    📅 Calendar
                                    Month / Week / DaySwitch views with the buttons in the toolbar.
                                    + EventAdd a one-time or repeating event. Repeat options: weekly, every 2 weeks, monthly, every weekday. Set an end date or leave blank for ongoing.
                                    + TaskAdd a recurring task. Assign to a project, pick who it belongs to, set a schedule (weekly days, interval, or one-time), and set an estimated duration.
                                    Shared automaticallyEverything you add here is shared instantly with your partner — no separate accounts to connect.
                                    Day viewClick any date to open the day panel. Shows events and tasks side by side. Filter by person with the buttons at the top.
                                    💰 Finances
                                    BudgetClick any dollar amount to edit it. Totals update live. Add rows with the + button at the bottom of each section.
                                    Savings & FundsEach fund has a goal and a saved amount. Edit the saved amount — the progress bar fills automatically.
                                    Loan TrackerEnter balance, rate, and monthly payment. It calculates payoff date, total interest, and progress bar. Hit "Mark Paid" each month.
                                    Spending TrackerLog expenses as they happen. Shows current month by default. Tap "Show all history" to see previous months.
                                    📋 Projects
                                    Project cardsTap the header to expand or collapse. State saves between sessions.
                                    + TaskEach project card has its own + Task button. Adds a task directly to that project.
                                    Edit taskTap the ✏️ pencil on any task to edit name, schedule, duration, or who it belongs to.
                                    Task columnsTasks are sorted into You, Partner, and Together columns automatically based on who you assigned them to.
                                    + New ProjectAdd your own project with a name and emoji. Shows up alongside the built-in ones.
                                    ✝️ Faith
                                    Daily RhythmEight traditional Catholic practices. Tap to check off. Resets every day. All eight appear in your Home schedule automatically.
                                    Evening ExamenTap "Begin" for a guided 5-step Ignatian examination of conscience. Saves your responses. Quote changes daily.
                                    Liturgical seasonBadge in the header updates automatically — Advent, Christmastide, Lent, Eastertide, Ordinary Time.
                                    ConfessionEnter the date you last went. Shows days since, turns red after 30 days.
                                    Plan of LifeYour written personal spiritual commitments. Add, check off, delete.
                                    Sacramental PathMilestone tracker. Click the dot to cycle through statuses: pending → active → done. Click text to edit.
                                    Mass readingsPulls from the USCCB API daily. Hit ↻ Refresh if it doesn't load.
                                    🐾 Pets · 🍽 Meals · 📝 Notes
                                    PetsAdd custom sections for each pet — schedules, checklists, vet notes. Every title and item is editable. Skip this tab entirely if it doesn't apply to you.
                                    MealsWeekly meal grid — click any cell to type. Grocery list below it. "Clear Week" resets the meal grid.
                                    NotesFree-form notepad. Title + body, assign to either or both. Saves automatically.
                                    ⚙️ Settings
                                    Names & colorsChange display names, initials, and accent colors for you and your partner.
                                    Day start / endSets the window the daily scheduler uses to slot tasks. Default 6am–10pm.
                                    Household codeShare this code so your partner can join the same household from their own device — you'll see the same data.
                                    Big Goal DateDrives the "Days to Goal" countdown on the home page — set it to a wedding, move date, or any milestone you're working toward.
